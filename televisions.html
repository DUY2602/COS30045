Tips to Reduce TV Energy Consumption

  • Choose 6+ star rated TVs for optimal efficiency
  • Utilize Eco/Energy Saving mode when available
  • Adjust brightness to match ambient lighting
  • Enable automatic sleep/standby timers
  • Turn off completely when not in use (avoid standby power)
  • Consider smaller screen sizes if space allows
  • Unplug peripherals when not in use
  • Choose LED over OLED or Plasma for energy savings
  • Reduce screen brightness by 20-30% when possible
  • Use smart power strips to eliminate phantom loads
